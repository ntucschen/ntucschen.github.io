WHAT WE DO

IR Robotics

Image rigistration, disease investigation and face tracking based on Infrared thermal imaging and robotic instruments.

To develop and utilize medical devices combined with thermal imaging for improving illness condition surveying and postoperative monitoring.

Lung CAD

Computer-Aided Diagnosis (CAD) and Artificial Intelligence in Pulmonary Medical Imaging.

To develop computer-aided intelligence system in pulmonary medical imaging for helping to achieve optimal personalized diagnosis and treatment planning.

Heart A.I.

Analysis and Prognosis in Cardiovascular Disease.

To develop image processing with artificial intelligence system in coronary computed tomography angiography for preoperative planning and clinical appliance.
